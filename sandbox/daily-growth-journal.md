## 🤖 AI Learning Section

### Today's Practice Prompt
Learn by doing! Try asking Copilot one of these to get better with AI:

**Beginner:**
- "Can you help me think deeper about [my answer above]?"
- "What follow-up questions should I ask myself about [topic]?"
- "Can you explain [concept] in simple terms?"

**Intermediate:**
- "I'm thinking about [my thought]. Can you play devil's advocate and challenge it?"
- "Help me break down [problem] into smaller pieces I can solve."
- "What would a different perspective on this be?"

**Advanced:**
- "Create three scenarios where [my belief] might not be true. What would I learn?"
- "What would a coach, scientist, and artist each think about [my situation]?"
- "Help me find the assumptions I'm making about [topic]."

### AI Tip of the Day
- **Ask follow-up questions** - Don't accept the first answer. Ask "Why?", "How?", "What if?"
- **Ask for examples** - "Can you give me 3 real-world examples of this?"
- **Ask Copilot to challenge you** - "What questions haven't I asked myself?"
- **Compare perspectives** - "How would [person/expert] view this differently?"
